About PSIT

Since its inception in 2004, PSIT has been a frontrunner in the field of education with numerous achievements to its name. PSIT believes in strengthening the foundation of the students. Academic research based on rigorous pedagogy equips our students to face challenges in the real world. The institute prepares students to be ethical leaders in a diverse and complex world. PSIT offers various courses in Engineering, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Business Management and Computer Applications. All departments, with their dynamic community of students and distinguished faculty, offer premium undergraduate and postgraduate programs. State of the art facilities and a sprawling 80 acres campus nestled amid sylvan surroundings accelerate the learning process. The PSIT campus is an architectural marvel. Designed as the best environment-friendly campus in North India, we are committed to providing our students a clean and healthy environment. A 25,000 Ltr RO Plant, a 1350 KW Power Plant and a three-chambered Vermicompost Plant reaffirm our commitment to the cause. Our infrastructure which includes an auditorium, modern workshops, high-tech labs, spacious seminar halls and well stocked libraries can compete with the best campuses. Internet access at 1 Gbps (STM -1), a 24×7 Power Backup enabled through 33KV Power Line fuel our thirst for knowledge. Our fleet of 30 air-conditioned buses ensures an efficient transportation network. The institute provides a stimulating environment for intellectual development, free-thinking, and personal growth. The academic setup challenges its students with dynamic learning opportunities. We equip them with the skills, insights, attitudes, and practical experiences to take up responsibilities in their careers and life. PSIT owes its commitment to excellence to its illustrious faculty. A stringent selection process of faculty members is the cornerstone of our success. The exceptionally talented and seasoned teaching fraternity motivates the students to push boundaries and become the best versions of themselves. The annual placement portfolio of PSIT is a testimony to its excellent track record of outstanding placements through which our students are ready to carve a niche for themselves in the professional world. PSIT prepares its students to establish themselves in their chosen field of academics with an aim to develop well-rounded and discerning citizens.

VISION

To achieve excellence in professional education and create an ecosystem for the holistic development of all stakeholders.

Mission

To provide an environment of effective learning and innovation transforming students into dynamic, responsible and productive professionals in their respective fields, who are capable of adapting to the changing needs of the industry and society.
